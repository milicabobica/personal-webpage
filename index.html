About Me

Short introduction to my life, and my education. What makes me today, what I am.

Biography

My name is Mila Bozhinoska, born on September 29th, 2005, in Skopje, but raised in Tetovo. I am the youngest sibling of three children in a family full of engineers (except my sister, but I would say medicine is also good choice). My whole life, I've been interested in different things, no matter the field, if it's in the scientific part or the more artsy field. That shaped me to be curious and to try out hobbies that helped me grow into myself. I entered piano contests, art and photography contests, mathematical, chemistry, and biological contest. And, know more recently, a few hackathons.
⇐ Three generations of 'Finkashi'. (My brother, my cousin and me)

Education

My education began when I was 5 years old. Baby-faced and ready to enter the big world. My elementary school was the biggest school in the country, and that allowed me to start my journey with language studying. My first foreign languages were English and then French. But later on, I also started learning German. During my elementary years, I was quite active in sports such as swimming, tennis and volleyball. My next passage of education was in the high school "Georgi Dimitrov". There I was active in workshops, math contests, and charity work. And, now, starting from the year 2024, I am officially a student of the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering.
